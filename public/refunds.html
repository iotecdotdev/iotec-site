Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-17

Our refund policy differs between iotec Core (one-time purchase) and iotec GEX (monthly subscription). Read the relevant section.

iotec Core — 14-day money-back guarantee

If iotec Core does not work for you within 14 days of purchase, email us at soporte@iotec.dev and we will issue a full refund — no questions asked. After 14 days, refunds are evaluated case-by-case (significant unfixed bugs may qualify; "changed my mind" generally does not).

iotec GEX — 7-day free trial + 7-day refund

iotec GEX includes a 7-day free trial with no card required. You can fully evaluate the service before any charge. If you don't add a payment method, access stops automatically — no charge ever happens.

After the trial, if you decide it's not for you, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on the first paid charge. Email us within 7 days of being charged for the first time and we refund the full first month. Cancel anytime; subsequent monthly charges are non-refundable, but cancellation stops future billing immediately.

Process (both products)

  1. Email soporte@iotec.dev with the email used at purchase and your order/subscription number.
  2. We acknowledge within 24-48 h.
  3. Refund is processed via Lemon Squeezy (the payment processor). Money arrives back at your card in 3-10 business days, depending on your bank.
  4. Your license is deactivated. Please uninstall the Software from your system.

What is NOT refundable

  • Losses incurred while trading using iotec products. See the Risk Disclaimer.
  • iotec GEX subscription periods past the first paid month (cancel to stop future charges).
  • Custom development work (if separately contracted).
  • Licenses terminated due to breach of Terms (e.g., key sharing, reverse-engineering).
