Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-17

14-day money-back guarantee

If iotec's product does not work for you within 14 days of purchase, email us at soporte@iotec.dev and we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.

What "doesn't work" means

  • You couldn't install or activate it despite following our installation guide and contacting support for help.
  • It doesn't meet the functionality described on iotec.dev.
  • You changed your mind.
  • Any other reason within the 14-day window.

Process

  1. Email soporte@iotec.dev with the email used at purchase and your order number.
  2. We acknowledge within 24-48 h.
  3. Refund is processed via Lemon Squeezy (the payment processor). Money arrives back at your card in 3-10 business days, depending on your bank.
  4. Your license is deactivated. Please uninstall the Software from your system.

After 14 days

Refunds after 14 days are evaluated case-by-case. Significant bugs reported and not yet fixed may qualify; "changed my mind" generally does not.

What is NOT refundable

  • Losses incurred while trading using iotec products. See the Risk Disclaimer.
  • Custom development work (if separately contracted).
  • Licenses terminated due to breach of Terms (e.g., key sharing, reverse-engineering).
