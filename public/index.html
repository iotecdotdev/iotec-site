FAQ

Things people ask before buying.

Do I need NinjaTrader OrderFlow+ add-on?

No. iotec Core rebuilds the entire OF stack from raw tick data. You only need a regular NT8 license (free for sim, lifetime for live trading).

What versions of NinjaTrader are supported?

NinjaTrader 8 (any recent release with SharpDX bundled). Tested on 8.1.4+. We do not support NinjaTrader 7.

Can I install on multiple computers?

Each license binds to one device on first activation. If you need to move to a new PC (hardware upgrade, Windows reinstall, etc.), email support — 1 free device reset every 6 months.

What if the indicator doesn't work for me?

Email us within 14 days of purchase for a no-questions-asked refund.

How do I get updates?

When we release a new version, you receive an email with the changelog and updated download link. Your existing license continues working — no re-purchase.

Is the data sent to your server safe?

The indicator sends only your license key, an anonymized device hash, and the DLL hash for tamper detection. We never collect your trades, positions, watchlists, or chart configs.

What happens if your server goes down?

The indicator caches its license validation locally with a 72-hour grace period. So even if our servers are temporarily unreachable, you keep trading without interruption.

Can I get a custom indicator built?

Possibly. Email us with your spec and we'll quote.
