FAQ

Things people ask before buying.

Do I need NinjaTrader OrderFlow+ add-on?

No. iotec Core rebuilds the entire OF stack from raw tick data. You only need a regular NT8 license (free for sim, lifetime for live trading).

What versions of NinjaTrader are supported?

NinjaTrader 8 (any recent release with SharpDX bundled). Tested on 8.1.4+. We do not support NinjaTrader 7.

Can I install on multiple computers?

Each license binds to one device on first activation. If you need to move to a new PC (hardware upgrade, Windows reinstall, etc.), email support — 1 free device reset every 6 months.

What if the indicator doesn't work for me?

For iotec Core (one-time): email us within 14 days of purchase for a no-questions-asked refund. For iotec GEX (subscription): your 7-day free trial lets you test before any charge — no card required to start.

Why is iotec GEX a subscription instead of one-time?

iotec GEX depends on a live options data feed and server-side calculations we maintain continuously. Unlike Core (which runs entirely on your machine after you buy it), GEX requires ongoing infrastructure — paid data providers, low-latency servers, daily Schwab token refresh. The monthly fee covers all of that. You get the NT8 indicator included as part of the service.

What happens if I cancel iotec GEX?

Your access to the GEX service stops at the end of your current billing period. The indicator stays installed in NinjaTrader but reports as inactive — no data flows. You can re-subscribe at any time and it resumes instantly with the same license key.

How does the 7-day free trial work?

Sign up, paste your license key in NinjaTrader, and you're in. No credit card required at the start. On day 7 we email you a payment link to continue. If you don't pay, your access stops — that's it. No silent charges, no surprises.

How do I get updates?

When we release a new version, you receive an email with the changelog and updated download link. Your existing license continues working — no re-purchase for Core, no service interruption for GEX subscribers.

Is the data sent to your server safe?

The indicator sends only your license key, an anonymized device hash, and the DLL hash for tamper detection. We never collect your trades, positions, watchlists, or chart configs.

What happens if your server goes down?

The indicator caches its license validation locally with a 72-hour grace period. So even if our servers are temporarily unreachable, you keep trading without interruption.

Can I get a custom indicator built?

Possibly. Email us with your spec and we'll quote.
