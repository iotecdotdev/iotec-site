Onboarding — Step-by-step installation

Last updated: 2026-05-20

This guide takes you from zero to a working iotec indicator on NinjaTrader 8 in about 15 minutes. It applies whether you arrived via a purchase, a trial license, or beta access.

License types

The email you received tells you which type of license you have:

Purchase

Permanent license, no expiration date.

Beta

90 days free + 50% lifetime discount at launch.

Trial

14 days to test the product. No card, no commitment.

Prerequisites

  • NinjaTrader 8 installed (free from ninjatrader.com).
  • Windows 10 or 11 with a stable internet connection.
  • For iotecGEX and iotecGEXLab: Charles Schwab account with Developer Portal access and a registered app with permissions to Trader API - Market Data.
  • The welcome email with your license key and the Download binary button.

Installation steps

1Download the binary

Click the Download binary button in the email you received. The link is unique to your license and valid as long as your license is active. Save the .zip to your downloads folder.

If the link stops working, email soporte@iotec.dev — we'll regenerate access immediately.

2Close NinjaTrader 8 completely

Including the tray icon (right-click → Exit). Without this step, files can stay locked during the copy.

3Import the indicator

Open NinjaTrader 8 → Tools → Import → NinjaScript Add-On. Select the downloaded .zip. NT8 processes it automatically and may prompt you to restart the editor.

If NT8 reports compile errors, open the NinjaScript Editor (F5 compiles) and check the Errors tab. The most common cause is not having closed NT8 first (step 2).

4Activate your license

Open any chart, add the indicator (Indicators → search iotec…). The first time, a dialog will ask for:

  • License key: copy it exactly from the email (no extra spaces).
  • iotecGEX/iotecGEXLab only: Schwab App Key, App Secret and Callback URL from your app at the Schwab Developer Portal.

5Schwab OAuth (iotecGEX / iotecGEXLab only)

After you enter the credentials, an embedded window opens with the Schwab login. Sign in normally and authorize the app. The window closes itself when done. Tokens are stored encrypted (AES-256) on your machine — they never touch our servers.

If the window doesn't open or reports an Edge/WebView2 error, install the WebView2 runtime from microsoft-edge/webview2.

6Done — start using it

Load SPY, QQQ, /ES or /NQ on a chart. The GEX levels (or the metrics corresponding to your product) appear automatically. Configure range, DTE and filters via right-click → Properties.
